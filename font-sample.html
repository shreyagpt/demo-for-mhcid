A few of my favorite dogs:

American Eskimos are one of the most loyal dogs ever. They love following you around, but often get lonely if attention is not given.

Pomeranians are pretty awesome too. They usually come in many sizes, such as toy and medium. However, they can get really barky! Beware of the small dog syndrome.

Corgis are also pretty cute. Notorious for their loaf-like appearance, corgis are very cute. Unfortunately, they can have severe back issues later in life.
